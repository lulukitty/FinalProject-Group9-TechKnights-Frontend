Finding out more about your child school life and what they are learning shows your child how much you care about their education. And getting to know the school and the teachers is the best way of finding out more. Therefore being present in their opening day or a PTA meeting is a great way to support your kids.You get to meet their teacher and also know their performance in school.Also if they have a special need in learning or behavioural needs, you get to know by attending school events.
Most times as Parent we strugle with time management,work and other issues and despite our good intentions,it feels like we don't really have time for our kids and most times get to miss a lot of important information regarding them.

With Techknight we make things easier by letting you know about your child activities in school, their performances and where they might be needing assistance.That way they do better Academically and behaviourally.

Keep in mind that you can request meetings with their Teachers,view their performance and also know the next school event coming up.Whatever your situation , it is never too soon (or too late) to start helping your child develop a positive attitude towards learning.

Often times the more support a child gets from family,the more he or she will learn at school. By getting in touch with their teachers and being aware of their Academic performances, you get to know the area they need support and the best way you can reach out to them.This can give your child far greater goals and inspire them to try their best where they can.

Unlike other kids,some children require special attention and care.As parent one of our duty is to enquire about their situation and find the best way to help them
A special needs child may require alternative approaches to education that not only accommodate their conditions but also work toward creating ways for them to further their own capacity to learn and develop.Parent and teacher have a huge role to play as this kids may require expertise.While public education is open to all students, not all teachers are trained to present lessons in ways that speak to these needs. This may lead to them being taught privately,in schools dedicated to addressing those needs.
